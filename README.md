#PeerConnect — University of Zululand
Final-Year Project (2025) — Group 12

Team Lead: M. M. Mkhize

PeerConnect is a final-year capstone project developed by Group 12 (2025) at the University of Zululand, led by MM Mkhize.
The platform is designed to help students connect academically, share knowledge, and support one another through a modern peer-to-peer learning ecosystem.

 Project Overview

PeerConnect is a student academic support platform that enables:

Peer-to-peer tutoring

Secure messaging between students

Sharing of study materials

Academic discussions by module

AI-powered moderation for safe communication

Module & department-based academic networking

The system solves key challenges at universities, such as:

Limited access to affordable academic support

LMS platforms that prevent cross-level student interaction

Difficulty in finding peers who excel in specific modules

Lack of centralized communication for academic help
